## Overview

Arseniy Finberg provides a centralized platform for you to create, organize, and manage all your project documentation. You can build rich, interactive docs using MDX with components like Cards, Steps, and Tabs. This space supports teams and individuals by offering searchable content, version history, and seamless collaboration.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes with these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://dashboard.example.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click "New Documentation Space" and name it "My Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Doc" icon="file-text">
    Create `introduction.mdx` and add frontmatter:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: My First Doc
    description: Welcome to my documentation.
    ---
    
    ## Hello World
    
    This is your first page.
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Preview and publish to share your live docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

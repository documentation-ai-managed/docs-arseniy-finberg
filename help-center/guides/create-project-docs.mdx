## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Visit `https://arseniyfinberg.com/dashboard` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Find and press the 'New Project' button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in project details">
    Enter a name and description. Add relevant team members if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and start documenting">
    Press 'Create' and begin adding pages or sections for your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>

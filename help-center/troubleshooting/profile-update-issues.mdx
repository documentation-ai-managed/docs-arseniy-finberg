## Problem
- Profile changes don't save
- Error: "Unable to update profile"

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check required fields">
    Ensure all required fields (e.g., name, email) are filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and log in again">
    Sometimes a fresh session solves sync issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, email `support@arseniyfinberg.com` with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
